Getting and keeping in touch

Slack

We use the PowSyBl Slack for all chat. This is the first place to come if you think you've found a bug in PowSyBl or if you want to suggest an enhancement. You can also join to discuss or ask any question about PowSyBl.

Mailing lists

Subscribe to the following mailing lists so you don't miss out on the latest news:
  • General: general mailing list. This is also where new releases are announced;
  • Technical Steering Committee: reminders for TSC meetings and sharing of TSC slides. See also here to learn more about TSC meetings.
  • Open RAO: reminders for Open RAO meetings and sharing of slides. See also here to learn more about Open RAO meetings.
