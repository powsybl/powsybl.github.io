Regular meetings

The TSC meetings

The Technical Steering Committee (TSC) meetings are held on Zoom every month.

In order to receive updates on the TSC meetings, please susbscribe to the powsybl-tsc list.

TSC meetings are open to everyone. The agenda consists of updates and discussions relating to the project's organization or technical direction. Votes are organized when needed.

In order to learn more about how the TSC works, have a look at the Technical Steering Committee section on the Governance page.

The Open RAO meetings

The Open RAO sub-project holds dedicated public meetings. All contributors, users, and potential users are welcome to join and discuss RAO-specific topics.

When a technical decision must be made, Open RAO committers have authority within the scope of the sub-project. If reaching consensus proves particularly difficult, the TSC has the final say.

The list of Open RAO committers is maintained and made publicly available on the PowSyBl maintainers page.

These meetings take place on the last Thursday of every other month (odd-numbered months), and last two hours.

Viktor Terrier chairs these meetings. One Open RAO committer reports back to the TSC at its next meeting.

You can subscribe to these meetings and to the dedicated mailing list here.

If you want to add topics to the agenda, you can contact Viktor (viktor.terrier@rte-france.com). You will then be asked at the following meeting to present your topic.

The sub-project follows PowSyBl's project-wide governance.
